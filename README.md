# Snake Game

A simple Snake game built with Flutter and Flame engine.

## Application Description

**Snake Game** is a classic arcade-style game where players control a green snake to collect red food items on a dark background. The objective is to grow the snake by eating food while avoiding collisions with walls and the snake's own body.

### Purpose

The game provides an engaging and nostalgic gaming experience with multiple levels of increasing difficulty. Players can progress through levels by achieving target scores, with their progress saved between game sessions.

### Gameplay Instructions

1. **Start Screen**: Tap "Start Game" to begin
2. **Level Selection**: Choose from available unlocked levels
3. **Controls**:
   - **Swipe** in any direction on the game area to control the snake
   - **Tap directional buttons** (UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT) at the bottom
4. **Objective**: Collect red food items to increase your score
5. **Win Condition**: Reach the target score for your level
6. **Game Over**: Avoid hitting walls or the snake's own body

### Level Progression

- **Level 1 (Beginner)**: Score 10 points, slower speed
- **Level 2 (Intermediate)**: Score 15 points, medium speed
- **Level 3 (Advanced)**: Score 20 points, fast speed

Each level must be completed to unlock the next one. Your progress is automatically saved.
